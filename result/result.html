What is the highest governing body of basketball?
  1. EuroLeague
  2. NBA

FIBA, or the International Basketball Federation, is an association of the different national basketball organizations.

When would a player be awarded a free throw?
  1. When they make a shot from the half-court.
  2. When they are fouled while shooting.
  3. When they perform an assist.

If the ball-handler is fouled while attempting to make a shot into the net, they are awarded a free throw.

What is a turnover?
  1. When the two teams switch.
  2. The different ways that possession of the ball goes from one team to another.
  3. A player is transferred to the other team during a game.

A turnover is any of the various ways that possession of the ball goes from one team to another.

What does BEEF mean?
  1. Balance, Eyes, Elbow, Follow Through
  2. Basketball, Elbow, Endline, Free-Throw
  3. Box and One, Euro Step, Elevator Screen, Field Goal

BEEF is an acronym used by coaches to show proper shooting technique. It stands for “Balance, Eyes, Elbow, and Follow Through.”

What is a technical foul?
  1. A foul that comes from significant physical contact
  2. A foul unrelated to physical contact.
  3. A foul that occurs when neither team is in clear possession of the ball

Technical fouls do not relate to physical contact.
